    About

Walk into this mysterious world, side by side with Arsen. Observe paintings role playing. Dance with dolls. Experience thousands of lives in cartoons.     About Our Works

    Motion Graphics

    Motion graphics is animation, but with text as a major component. Essentially, it’s animated graphic design. Motion graphics are a way to communicate with the viewer. Together with music and effective copy, they can give us a message. We use them to create ads, title sequences for movies and videos to share information. Motion graphics are usually displayed via electronic media technology, but may also be displayed via manual powered technology (e.g. thaumatrope, phenakistoscope, stroboscope, zoetrope, praxinoscope, flip book). Many of our templates use motion graphics to help you communicate your message.

    Digital Paint

    Concept art is the creation of ideas for the bigger and final product, which can be video game, or a movie, or even a commercial market. Indeed, when there is a need for imagination, concept art is there to help.

    3D fashion design

    3D fashion design Is based on a pattern, which is consist of smaller pieces of pattern, which is sewn together to create a dress. 3D fashion design is an standard way of designing in fashion industry which is used for virtual dress design, as well. A dress is made up of separate patterns, then assembled on a 3D avatar, so the designer has a flexible collection of clothes which can be used in the future. This method is used by virtual fashion designers, game and animation studios, and clothing companies.
